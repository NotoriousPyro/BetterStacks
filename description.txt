BetterStacks mod by NotoriousPyro

This mod makes the following changes to stack sizes:

Increased from 999 to 9999:
* All Blocks, Arrows, Bullets, Walls, Fences, Ropes, Bottles, Gels, Paints, Platforms, Shuriken, Cobwebs, Bones, Wood

Increased from 99 to 999:
* All Acorns, Torches, Bars, Hooks, Grenades and Bombs

Increased to 99:
* All Potions, Dynamite

Any suggestions? Let me know!

Mod page: https://pyronexus.com/terraria-itemstack
