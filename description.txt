BetterStacks mod by NotoriousPyro

This mod makes the following changes to stack sizes:

Increased to 9999:
* All Arrows, Blocks, Bone Knives, Bones, Bottles, Bullets, Cobwebs, Fences, Gels, Musket Balls, Paints, Platforms, Ropes, Shuriken, Throwing Knives, Walls, Wood

Increased to 999:
* All Acorns, Bars, Bombs, Grenades, Hooks, Tissue Sample, Torches, Turtle Scale, Vertebrae

Increased to 99:
* All Dynamite, Potions

Any suggestions? Let me know!

Mod page: https://pyronexus.com/terraria-itemstack
Source: https://github.com/NotoriousPyro/BetterStacks
